Who We Are

TiffinFinder is a user-friendly online platform designed to bridge the gap between local tiffin/mess services and people looking for daily, hygienic, and affordable meals. Whether you're a student, working professional, or just someone in need of homemade food, we make it easy for you to discover and connect with verified mess providers in your area.

Our Mission

  • To empower local mess owners by giving them digital visibility.
  • To help customers find reliable and healthy meal options quickly and easily.
  • To create a community-driven food discovery platform focused on trust, quality, and convenience.

What We Do

  • List and promote verified mess/tiffin providers.
  • Let customers browse menus, prices, and reviews before choosing a service.
  • Provide location-based search to show mess options near you.
  • Enable direct contact or lead generation between customer and provider.
Note: We do not cook or deliver food. We simply connect those who offer meals with those who need them.
